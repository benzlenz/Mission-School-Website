About Our School

Our Dream

Blessing English Medium School was founded with the dream of providing quality education to the children of Bhachau and surrounding areas. Our dream is to create an institution that nurtures young minds, fosters creativity, and prepares students to face the challenges of the modern world while staying rooted in traditional values.

Our Principal

Mrs. Betsy Blessen, with over 20 years of experience in education, leads our school with vision and dedication. She holds a Master's degree in Education and has been instrumental in implementing innovative teaching methodologies. Under her leadership, the school has consistently achieved academic excellence while maintaining a focus on holistic development.

Our Chairman

Mr. Blessen Mathew Sam, a prominent educationist and philanthropist, provides strategic direction to the institution. His vision of affordable quality education has shaped the school's policies and growth. With his guidance, the school has expanded its infrastructure and academic offerings while maintaining its commitment to accessibility.

Our Founders

Mr. and Mrs. Wakerly established Blessing English Medium School in 1995 with a modest beginning. Their dedication to education and community service laid the foundation for what has become one of the most respected educational institutions in the region. Their legacy continues to inspire the school's mission and values.

Admission Information

Admission Procedure

Admissions are open for the academic year 2023-24. The admission process is conducted both offline and online.

Offline Procedure:

  1. Visit the school office between 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM on working days
  2. Collect the admission form and prospectus
  3. Submit the completed form along with required documents
  4. Pay the admission fees to confirm the seat

Documents Required:

  • Birth Certificate (for new admissions)
  • Transfer Certificate (for students coming from other schools)
  • Aadhar Card of student and parents
  • Previous year's report card
  • Passport size photographs (4 copies)

Payment Options

We offer flexible payment options to suit parents' convenience:

Online Payment:

  • Through our secure payment gateway on the school website
  • UPI payment to school's UPI ID: blessing@upi
  • Net Banking/NEFT/RTGS

Offline Payment:

  • Cash payment at school office
  • Cheque/Demand Draft in favor of "Blessing English Medium School"

Fee structure varies according to grade level. Please contact the office for detailed fee information.

Scholarships

The school facilitates various scholarships provided by the Gujarat government:

1. Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavlamban Yojana

For meritorious students of class 11-12 with family income less than 6 lakhs per annum.

2. Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya

For girls from SC/ST/OBC/minority communities for free education and hostel facilities.

3. Praveshotsav Scholarship

For students securing more than 80% in board exams continuing to higher education.

4. EBC Post-Matric Scholarship

For economically backward class students with family income less than 1 lakh per annum.

For more details and application procedures, please contact our scholarship cell.

Our Facilities

Bus Facility

Our school operates five modern buses with a capacity of 80 students each, covering all major routes in Bhachau and surrounding areas. All buses are equipped with GPS tracking, first aid kits, and trained attendants for student safety.

Computer Lab

Our state-of-the-art computer lab features 40 high-speed computers with the latest software, high-speed internet, and interactive learning tools. Students receive hands-on training in programming, digital literacy, and computer applications.

Chemistry Lab

The well-equipped chemistry lab allows students to perform experiments safely under expert supervision. It contains all necessary chemicals, apparatus, and safety equipment to facilitate practical learning as per CBSE guidelines.

Maths Lab

Our innovative maths lab makes learning mathematics interactive and fun. With models, charts, and digital tools, students develop conceptual understanding through practical activities and real-world applications.

Sports

We have excellent sports facilities including a playground for cricket, football, and athletics; indoor facilities for table tennis, chess, and carrom; and specialized coaching in various sports. Regular inter-school competitions are organized.

Our Curriculum

HKG & UKG

Our kindergarten program focuses on holistic development through play-way methods. The curriculum includes:

  • Language development (English, Gujarati, Hindi)
  • Numeracy skills
  • Environmental awareness
  • Art and craft
  • Music and movement
  • Physical activities
  • Social skills development

1 to 8 (Primary)

The primary curriculum follows CBSE guidelines with emphasis on conceptual understanding:

  • English, Gujarati, Hindi (Languages)
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Studies (EVS)
  • Science (from Grade 6)
  • Social Studies
  • Computer Science
  • General Knowledge
  • Value Education
  • Art, Music, Physical Education

9 to 10 (Secondary)

Secondary curriculum prepares students for board examinations with rigorous academics:

  • English, Gujarati/Hindi (Languages)
  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Science (History, Geography, Civics, Economics)
  • Computer Applications/Information Technology
  • Additional Skill Subjects

11 & 12 (Higher Secondary)

We offer three streams in higher secondary with specialized focus:

Science Stream:

  • Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology
  • English, Gujarati/Hindi
  • Computer Science/Physical Education

Commerce Stream:

  • Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics
  • English, Gujarati/Hindi
  • Mathematics/Informatics Practices

Humanities Stream:

  • History, Political Science, Economics/Psychology
  • English, Gujarati/Hindi
  • Geography/Sociology

Examination System

Semester 1

Mid Term Examination

  • Conducted in September
  • Covers 50% of syllabus
  • 5 to 8 subjects depending on grade level
  • Duration: 2-3 hours per paper
  • Weightage: 20% of final grade

End Semester Examination

  • Conducted in December
  • Covers entire semester syllabus
  • 5 to 8 subjects depending on grade level
  • Duration: 2-3 hours per paper
  • Weightage: 30% of final grade

Semester 2

Mid Term Examination

  • Conducted in February
  • Covers 50% of syllabus
  • 5 to 8 subjects depending on grade level
  • Duration: 2-3 hours per paper
  • Weightage: 20% of final grade

End Semester Examination

  • Conducted in April (for classes 1-11)
  • Board exams for class 10 & 12 (March)
  • Covers entire syllabus
  • 5 to 8 subjects depending on grade level
  • Duration: 2-3 hours per paper
  • Weightage: 30% of final grade

Continuous Evaluation

In addition to term exams, students are assessed through:

  • Unit Tests (10% weightage)
  • Projects and Assignments (10% weightage)
  • Class Participation (5% weightage)
  • Practical Exams (for relevant subjects)

Grading System

We follow a comprehensive grading system:

  • A1: 91-100% (Outstanding)
  • A2: 81-90% (Excellent)
  • B1: 71-80% (Very Good)
  • B2: 61-70% (Good)
  • C1: 51-60% (Satisfactory)
  • C2: 41-50% (Acceptable)
  • D: 33-40% (Needs Improvement)
  • E: Below 33% (Unsatisfactory)

School Events

International Yoga Day

We celebrate International Yoga Day on June 21st every year with great enthusiasm. Students and teachers participate in mass yoga sessions, learning various asanas and their benefits. The event promotes physical fitness, mental well-being, and the ancient Indian tradition of yoga.

Industrial Visit

Our students regularly visit local industries and businesses to gain practical exposure. These visits help them understand real-world applications of their classroom learning. Recent visits include the Bhachau Industrial Estate, Kandla Port, and local agricultural processing units.

Republic Day Parade

Our annual Republic Day celebration features a grand parade by students, cultural performances, and patriotic songs. The event instills national pride and commemorates India's constitutional values. Students participate in marching drills, flag hoisting, and speeches about India's democratic heritage.

Annual Sports Day

Sports Day is a highlight of our academic calendar, showcasing students' athletic talents. Events include track and field competitions, team sports, and fun races. The day promotes sportsmanship, teamwork, and healthy competition among students.

Science Exhibition

Our annual science exhibition encourages innovation and scientific thinking. Students display working models, experiments, and research projects. The event attracts visitors from the community and provides a platform for young scientists to showcase their creativity.

Annual Day Celebration

The school's Annual Day is a grand cultural extravaganza featuring dances, dramas, music performances, and award ceremonies. Students showcase their talents while parents and community members celebrate their achievements.
